We launched a business-to-business (B2B) pilot project for selling polished diamonds. The project will allow businesses to sell their diamonds in a new, direct way. We will offer midstream diamond companies the chance to sell certain types of polished diamonds to other trade participants using this platform. A seller wishing to use the service will need to meet with several ethical requirements, including:
